OS X

Shell Access

  1. Open Spotligh Search by clicking the magnifing glass in the upper right of the screen.
  2. Search for the terminal, and open it.
  3. Type "ssh <csname>@bluenose.cs.dal.ca" and press enter, where <csname> is your csid
  4. Accept the key fingerprint (Won't be asked after first time)
  5. Type your CSID password and press enter (no characters will appear while you are typing)
  6. You can now issue shell commands to bluenose
